About Michael Ferrari

I help teams bring structure, clarity, recovery discipline, responsive web delivery, and executive polish to technical projects.

Technical delivery support for teams that need the plan, message, website, and launch path to be clear

My background is in leading complex workstreams, aligning stakeholders, building project structure, and translating raw information into leadership-ready communication and delivery plans.

I also bring hands-on development experience building simple, responsive HTML/CSS websites, supporting static site deployment, and helping teams move from idea to launch without unnecessary complexity.

I have experience supporting commercial, federal, and enterprise customer delivery environments, including high-value project work in the $7M-$10M range and situations where project health needed to move from red or yellow back toward green.

I work best with teams that are moving quickly but need stronger PM discipline, cleaner executive visibility, practical technical execution, better operating rhythm, and guidance around risks, decisions, owners, scope, and next steps.
